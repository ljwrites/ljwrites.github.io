On Gardening (種花)

This poem is by the renowned medieval Korean writer Lee Gyubo, and it’s where the current title of this blog (O but flowers fade) comes from.

種花

On Gardening

種花愁未發 花發又愁落
開落摠愁人 未識種花樂

I plant the seed and fear it may not bloom;
The flower blossoms, O but it will fade!
In bloom and fade I worry all the same;
My heart does but the garden’s joys evade.

— Lee Gyubo (李奎報), 13th century Korean essayist and poet
