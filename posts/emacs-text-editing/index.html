I love Emacs and I use it every day for a bunch of stuff, from text editing to email. I thought I’d talk a little about the uses I make They say Emacs is a great OS lacking only a decent text editor. Fortunately you can remedy the latter by getting an implementation of the best text editor, Vim, into Emacs through Evil Mode and its sister packages. Of course YMMV, ykinmk etc. etc., and not every Vim user finds Evil a usable implementation, but at my depth of use I find it reproduces much of what I love about Vim.
